Digi Mitra: Online Voter ID Card Services Guide

Voter ID card ya EPIC (Electors Photo Identity Card) India mein voting aur identity ke liye ek anivarya dastavez hai. Digi Mitra portal aapko NVSP ki sabhi mahatvapurn services ko ek hi jagah access karne ki suvidha deta hai.

Documents Checklist for New Voter ID

Naye Voter ID card ke liye apply karte waqt niche diye gaye dastavezon ki zarurat hoti hai:

  • Ayu Pramaan (Age Proof): Birth Certificate ya 10th Class Marksheet.
  • Pata Pramaan (Address Proof): Aadhaar Card, Ration Card, ya Electricity Bill.
  • Identity Proof: PAN Card ya Driving License.
  • Photo: Ek saaf Passport size photo jiska background white ho.

Digital Voter ID (E-EPIC) Kaise Download Karein?

Agar aapka Voter ID mobile number se link hai, toh aap asani se digital copy download kar sakte hain. Digi Mitra ke 'Download E-EPIC' card par click karein, login karein aur apna EPIC number dalkar OTP verify karein.
