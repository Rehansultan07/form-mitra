Online Voter ID Card Services: Complete Guide

Voter ID card, jise EPIC (Electors Photo Identity Card) bhi kaha jata hai, India mein voting ke liye sabse zaroori document hai. Form Mitra portal ke madhyam se aap NVSP (National Voters' Service Portal) ki sabhi services ko asani se access kar sakte hain.

Documents Required for New Voter ID

Agar aap pehli baar Voter ID ke liye apply kar rahe hain, toh aapko niche diye gaye documents ki scanned copy chahiye hogi:

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, ya Driving License.
  • Address Proof: Ration Card, Electricity Bill, ya Bank Passbook.
  • Photograph: Ek passport size photo (White background preferred).
  • Age Proof: 10th Class Certificate ya Birth Certificate (Agar aapki umar 18-21 saal hai).

How to Download E-EPIC Online?

Apna digital Voter ID card download karne ke liye in steps ko follow karein:

  1. NVSP portal par login karein (Agar account nahi hai toh Sign-up karein).
  2. "Download E-EPIC" section par click karein.
  3. Apna EPIC number ya Form Reference number dalein.
  4. Registered mobile number par aaye OTP ko verify karein.
  5. Aapka Voter ID PDF format mein download ho jayega.

Note: Form Mitra ek educational portal hai jo aapko direct Government websites tak pahunchane mein madad karta hai. Hum kisi bhi tarah ka personal data store nahi karte.
