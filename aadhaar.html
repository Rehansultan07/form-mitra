Aadhaar Online Services: Complete User Guide

Aadhaar card UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India) dwara jari kiya gaya ek 12-digit ka biometric identity document hai. Digi Mitra portal aapko UIDAI ke naye myAadhaar portal ki sabhi main services tak turant pahunchata hai.

Aadhaar Update Checklist

Agar aap Aadhaar mein koi sudhaar (Update) karwana chahte hain, toh in baaton ka dhyan rakhein:

  • Mobile Number: Online update ke liye aapka mobile number Aadhaar se link hona anivarya hai.
  • Address Update: Pata badalne ke liye aapko Ration Card, Electricity Bill, ya Voter ID ki zarurat hogi.
  • Biometric Lock: Apne data ko surakshit rakhne ke liye aap myAadhaar portal se biometric lock/unlock kar sakte hain.

Note: Digi Mitra ek independent portal hai. Hum kisi bhi prakar ka biometric data store nahi karte. Sabhi transactions UIDAI ki official website par hoti hain.
